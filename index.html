About Us

Welcome to Nachiketa Yoga, a sanctuary of peace and wellness nestled in the heart of Bopal, Ahmedabad. I am a super enthusiastic woman running yoga classes with flexible timings. Our goal is to inspire and empower individuals to achieve physical, mental, and spiritual well-being through the practice of yoga.

Our Mission

At Nachiketa Yoga, we are creating a nurturing space where everyone, regardless of age or experience, can explore the transformative power of yoga. We believe in fostering a welcoming community that promotes mindfulness, health, and harmony.

Our Philosophy

We are rooted in the belief that yoga is more than just a physical practice—it is a way of life. Our teachings integrate traditional yogic principles with modern techniques, focusing on breath, movement, and mindfulness. We aim to provide a holistic experience that supports your overall well-being.

Why Choose Us

What sets us apart is our commitment to personalized and accessible yoga practice. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced practitioner, our diverse range of classes, from Hatha to Vinyasa, are designed to meet you where you are.
